TED DUCHAMP

Ted Duchamp is a Stockholm four-piece mixing indie rock with a dash of jazz, a pinch of britpop and a splash of post‑punk. Think neon‑lit backstreets at dusk and palm silhouettes at dawn. Praised by Bond Magazine (US) and Oscar Lang (UK), the band has built a reputation as an explosive live act, opening for Junior Brielle. With Harry Todd (RAT BOY) producing the debut album, now’s your chance to catch their unreleased hits live.
